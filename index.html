About Me.

Hello!
I'm Thomas.

I'm a Franco-Italian international student based in Montreal, Quebec, Canada (45.5017° N, 73.5673° W). I'm currently finishing my third year at JMSB with a major in Business Technology Management. My curiosity pushed me to learn web-development during my summer break in 2020 with the intention of making a website from scratch.

Since then, I have made a few projects (mostly front-end websites), picked up a few languages, and learned a lot! Being a natural problem solver helped me through the tough times of debugging and countless hours of coping with missing semi-columns.

My future goals are to learn back-end development and master web-development as a whole. I'm also an aspiring business analyst consultant so having a hands-on approach will provide me knowing how to program will help me in the future!
